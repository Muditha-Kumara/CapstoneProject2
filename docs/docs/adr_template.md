Architecture Decision Record (ADR) Template

An Architecture Decision Record (ADR) documents important decisions made during the system's design and development.
Each ADR explains the context, the decision, and the consequences of that decision.

1. Title

A short title describing the decision.

Example:
"Choice of Frontend Framework"

2. Status

Proposed

Accepted

Deprecated

Replaced

Example:
Status: Accepted

3. Context

Describe the background, problem, or issue that led to the decision.

Example:

The team needed a framework that supports reusable UI components and fast development.

4. Decision

State the final decision clearly.

Example:

The team selected TailwindCSS for styling due to its utility-first approach and simplicity.

5. Alternatives Considered

List other options and why they were not chosen.

Example:

Bootstrap – heavier and less flexible

Material UI – more complex for a simple prototype

6. Consequences
Positive:

Faster UI development

Consistent styling

Negative:

Requires learning new utility classes

7. References

(Links or notes)

Example:

Tailwind documentation

Internal discussion notes

8. Summary

This template helps the team document why decisions were made and ensures future contributors understand the reasoning.
